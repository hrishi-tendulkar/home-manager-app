# Roadmap

This roadmap reflects current areas of focus and exploration. It will evolve as the product and understanding mature.

## Now
- Improve capture flows for home-related information (manual, photos, PDFs, conversational)
- Refine the core data model around services, occurrences, and follow-ups
- Polish core UX to keep the experience calm and low-friction

## Next
- Introduce lightweight reminders and follow-ups based on captured data
- Add smarter summaries and guidance using AI
- Explore proactive suggestions for common home maintenance needs

## Later
- Integrations with email, calendars, or external tools
- Deeper history, trends, and long-term insights
